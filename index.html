Item scorer
Your item scorer lives in Google Sheets so you and your partner can both fill it in at the same time from any device. Set it up once and reuse it for every purchase decision.
Step 1 Set up your Google Sheet

You need a sheet with columns for: product name, your score (1–10) per criteria, your partner's score, weights (%), and a final weighted average. The formula does the ranking automatically.

Open your Google Sheet
Step 2 Choose your criteria & weights

Decide what matters for each item you're comparing, and give each a weight that adds up to 100%. Both of you score each product 1–10 independently.

Suction power Maneuverability Aesthetics Noise level Price Storage size + add your own
Step 3 Read the winner

The sheet averages your scores, multiplies by the weight, and sums to give each product a final score out of 10. Highest score wins — no debates needed.
